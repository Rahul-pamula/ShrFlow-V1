## Upload Failed Completely
* **Problem:** The system rejects your file immediately.
* **Likely Reason:** You uploaded an Excel (`.xlsx`) file or an Apple Numbers file instead of a `.csv`.
* **Easiest Fix:** Open your spreadsheet, click `File > Save As`, and choose "Comma Separated Values (.csv)". Upload the new file.

## Missing Data
* **Problem:** Contacts imported, but names or emails are missing.
* **Likely Reason:** The top row of your spreadsheet does not have clear headers.
* **Easiest Fix:** Make sure the very first row of your spreadsheet says "Email" over the email column and "Name" over the name column. Re-upload and map the fields.
* **When to contact support:** If your file is a perfect `.csv` but the system continually freezes during import.
