Here are the most common issues you might run into and how to fix them quickly.

## Issue 1: Emails are not sending
* **Problem:** You clicked send, but nothing is happening.
* **Likely Reason:** Your sending domain is not verified, or you hit your monthly limit.
* **Easiest Fix:** Check **Settings > Domains** to ensure your domain has a green "Verified" badge.
* **When to contact support:** If your domain is verified and you are under your limit, but emails are still stuck after an hour.

## Issue 2: Analytics are delayed
* **Problem:** You sent a campaign, but opens and clicks are at zero.
* **Likely Reason:** Analytics process in large batches behind the scenes.
* **Easiest Fix:** Wait 5 to 10 minutes.
* **When to contact support:** If numbers are stuck at zero 1 hour after sending.

## Issue 3: Editor is lagging
* **Problem:** The email builder is slow.
* **Likely Reason:** Too many browser tabs open, or you uploaded massive images.
* **Easiest Fix:** Refresh the page and resize your images.
