## Domain Verification Failed
* **Problem:** You added your DNS records but ShrFlow still says "Pending" or "Failed."
* **Likely Reason:** A typo in the records, or the internet hasn't updated yet.
* **Easiest Fix:** Delete the records in your DNS provider and copy-paste them again directly from ShrFlow. Wait at least 1 hour.
* **When to contact support:** If verification still fails after 24 hours.

## Emails Going to Spam
* **Problem:** Your test emails are landing in the spam folder.
* **Likely Reason:** Your domain is not verified, or your subject line looks spammy (e.g., ALL CAPS).
* **Easiest Fix:** Ensure your domain is fully verified. Always include an "Unsubscribe" link in your footer.
* **When to contact support:** If you are following all best practices but still landing in spam across multiple providers.
