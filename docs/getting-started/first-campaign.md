**Goal:** Build and launch your first email blast.  
**Estimated Time:** 5 Minutes

## Step 1: Campaign Setup

<Frame>
  <img src="/docs/screenshots/campaign-builder.png" alt="Create New Campaign" />
</Frame>

* **What you should do:** Go to **Campaigns > New Campaign**. Add a Subject Line and select your verified domain.
* **Expected Result:** You are taken to the design step.
* **Common Mistake:** Forgetting to select an audience.

## Step 2: Design Your Email

<Frame>
  <img src="/docs/screenshots/templates-gallery.png" alt="Templates Gallery" />
</Frame>

* **What you should do:** Use the drag-and-drop builder to add text and images.
* **Expected Result:** Your email looks great on the preview screen.

## Step 3: Send a Test
* **What you should do:** Click **Send Test** and email it to yourself.
* **Expected Result:** The test email arrives in your inbox.
* **Troubleshooting Tip:** Check your spam folder if the test doesn't arrive within a minute.

## Step 4: Send Now
* **What you should do:** On the final screen, click **Send Now**.
* **Expected Result:** A success message confirms your campaign is sending.
