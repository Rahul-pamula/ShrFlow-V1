**Goal:** Send your first campaign.  
**Estimated Time:** 10 Minutes

<Frame>
  <img src="/screenshots/dashboard-checklist.png" alt="Launch Checklist" />
</Frame>

## 1. Login to Your Workspace
* **What you should do:** Go to the login page and sign in with your work email.
* **Expected Result:** You will see your dashboard.
* **Common Mistake:** Using a personal email instead of your invited work email.

## 2. Verify Your Domain
* **What you should do:** Go to **Settings > Domains** and add your website to generate SPF and DKIM records.
* **Expected Result:** A green "Verified" badge appears.
* **Troubleshooting Tip:** If it stays pending, DNS updates can take up to 24 hours.

## 3. Import Contacts

<Frame>
  <img src="/screenshots/contacts-page.png" alt="Contacts Management" />
</Frame>

* **What you should do:** Go to **Audience > Import** and upload your CSV file.
* **Expected Result:** Contacts are imported successfully.
* **Common Mistake:** Forgetting to include an "Email" column in your spreadsheet.

## 4. Send a Campaign
* **What you should do:** Go to **Campaigns > New Campaign**. Design your email and hit Send.
* **Expected Result:** Your campaign is queued and delivered.
